Music Video Builder

Music video builder allows you to combine clips to either make a new video or edit an existing one.

Once you create a video in the editor then you can download the assets to create your video on your machine. Use the controls below to create or edit your videos

    When you have downloaded your zip then you can build your video using ffmpeg. If you do not have ffmpeg then you will need to do this first.

    1. Extract your zip and make note of it's location.
    2. Using your favourite command line tool, go to the location of the extracted zip.
    3. Open the command.txt document and copy the ffmpeg command it contains.
    4. Paste the command in your command line tool and hit enter.
