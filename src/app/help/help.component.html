The frame rate will be 24 frames per second. As the videos are synced up to the right bpm then there is no need for a higher framerate.

Video files will be kept for 7 days before automatic deletion.

You can only be building 2 videos at one time however this will not stop you from editing a building video. This is done deliberately to stop spamming the build process.

Audio must be compatible with the browser so mp3 is a format that will work more universally. There is a 40MB limit for the sake of upload time when it comes to building your video.

Understandably if you wish to have high quality audio then make the video with an mp3 version then you can replace the audio easily yourself with video editing software or with ffmpeg using this commad:

ffmpeg -i /path/to/video/file.mp4 -i /path/to/audio/file.wav -acodec copy -vcodec copy -map 0:v:0 -map 1:a:0 combined_file.mp4

There is a 15 minute limit to videos. Most songs are not longer than this. If you are determined to get past this limit then build across multiple videos and stick the videos together afterwards in a video editor.
