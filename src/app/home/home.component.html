Music Video Builder is dedicated to creating videos for music

Why?

We are fed up with seeing static images on youtube and dull backgrounds on dj sets. We want to see better videos for music creators.

Who?

If you have a music file with constant Beats Per Minute then you can use this to create your own video.

How?

Music Video Builder works by providing content with online tools to compose videos. The aim is that you end up owning layers that you can reuse in multiple videos.

There are 3 easy steps to create your video:

First you must have an account with us so you'll need to sign in using the top right button, we only need a username and email to save your work against.

  1. Use Content Browser to browse our content collections. No login required for browsing.
  2. Use Clip Builder to customise, combine and create transitions from our content collections to create your unique clips. Login required in order to save you changes.
  3. Use Music Video Builder to combine your clips with your music file to create your video. Login required in order to save you changes.
