Music Video Builder is dedicated to creating videos for music

What?

This site allows you to sequence customised clips perfectly in time with your constant bpm audio. The end result being a custom video file generated for the user.

Why?

We are fed up with seeing static images on youtube and dull backgrounds on dj sets. Not everyone has the resources to fund a fully fledged custom video for every song they produce, we aim to bridge that gap.

Who?

VJs, DJs, producers, creators; if you have a music file with constant beats per minute and need some low hassle content then this is for you.

How?

Music Video Builder works by providing content with online tools to compose videos. Content comes in the shape of collections which will contain various directions and shapes to choose from. We've created a pricing model where you can purchase a collection license once and not be charged for further customisations in new videos using our modifiers

Watch the tutorial or scroll to the steps below to see the website in action.

There are 3 steps to create your video:

  1. Use Content Browser to browse our content collections. This allows you to see if we have desirable content with no obligation to sign up.
  2. Use Clip Builder to customise, combine and create transitions from our content collections to create your unique clips. Login required in order to save your changes.
  3. Use Music Video Builder to combine your clips with your music file to create your video. Login required in order to save your changes.
