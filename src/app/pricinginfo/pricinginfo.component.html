To work out your total for a video you need to decide which resolution and license you need. Then you take the license cost muliplied by the number of collections and add it to the build cost.

If I wanted a HD standard license for a video which used collection X and collection Y then my total would be (B is build cost):

X + Y + B = {{pricingService.pricePerCollection(License.Standard, Resolution.Hd) | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}} + {{pricingService.pricePerCollection(License.Standard, Resolution.Hd) | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}} + {{pricingService.pricePerBuild(Resolution.Hd) | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}} = {{pricingService.total(License.Standard, Resolution.Hd, 2, null) | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}}

Once you have a license at a given resolution for a collection then you will no longer be charged license fees.

Lets continue the example assuming you paid for the video. Now you modify the video to have an extra collection Z then under the same license and reolution the total would be:

X + Y + Z + B = {{0 | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}} + {{0 | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}} + {{pricingService.pricePerCollection(License.Standard, Resolution.Hd) | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}} + {{pricingService.pricePerBuild(Resolution.Hd) | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}} = {{pricingService.total(License.Standard, Resolution.Hd, 1, null) | currency:"GBP":"symbol"}}

There are also Subscriptions that can eliminate the build and license costs when active.

If you have an active license or license builder subscription then you won't pay the license fee for any collection on the site. Your license will be valid as long as your subscription is active. The builder and license builder subscription works similarily, eliminating build costs.
